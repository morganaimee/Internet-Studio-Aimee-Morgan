What We're All About

Here at Free Library Access, we believe in going the extra mile to help people find books they love, for free. The US is experiencing a literary crisis. We are doing our part to provide access to literature and a variety of other online resources, so if you struggle to find something to read easily, we hope to make it a bit easier.
