Favorite Movies Gallery

I like all of the following movies because I love strong characters, friends to lovers, women as main characters, princesses, and most of all, adventure and the theme of love prevailing above all else. These movies are varied in their themes, but they please me all the same.

Ratatouille


A rat enchants humans with his talent and becomes a great chef.

Soul


A man dies and sees all of the what-ifs of his life, questions about the meaning of life ensue.

The Incredibles


Family of superheroes fight off evil with the help of their friends.

Sinners


A fun night at a party turns into a brutal fight to survive against vampires.

Wicked


A diva and a smart shy girl meet at school and one of them is green. They are friends and things get crazy.

Shrek 2


Shrek goes to meet his in-laws, insanity occurs instead.

Pride and Prejudice


Jane meets the most arrogant, annoying man, falls in love, as does the audience, thank you Mr. Darcy.

Coraline


Girl gets fed up with parents and life, discovers she has another reality where her parents and life are perfect, or so it seems.

The Princess Diaries


Awkward girl with glasses and curls finds out she's a princess and suddenly has straight hair and contacts, also is now rich and cool to everyone.

Tangled


Princess has crazy kidnapper that hides her away in a tower, charming man breaks in and leads her to freedom, kind of.

WALL-E


Earth is trashed, WALL-E is trying to help, he is the last of his kind. Fat people in space are lazy and nearly braindead, but together they return to Earth, somehow.

Beauty and the Beast


Beast steals girl to punish her father for intruding in his space, they fall in love, it is weird and great.
