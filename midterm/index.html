Take a Trip to the Swiss Alps

Gimmelwald is a small, traffic-free village in Switzerland. Locals farm and host tourists as their primary sources of income. During the winter, there are opportunities for skiing.

There are several hiking trails around the village. The only way to access the village is by cable car or hiking. Gimmelwald is the best place to reconnect with nature and disconnect from your phone.
